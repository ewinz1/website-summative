Samdoesarts

Samdoesarts is a youtube-based digital artist living in Toronto. His unique style consists of many different characteristics, such as angular facial features and high-contrast, stylized lighting. Something remarkable about Sam's style is that he can combine artistic elements from realism and cartoon techniques without letting his drawings look artificial or forced. All of his characters, who are mainly female, look natural and elegant. Learn more about Sam here.
